Counter-Terrorism Policies and Plans for building owners and managers

In the same way that a business should have a fire plan, a business should be in a position, with trained staff, to respond to a terrorist attack.

This means having a clear Counter-Terrorism Policy and an accessible Counter-Terrorism Plan which informs and instructs users and stake holders to help them respond effectively to an attack is vitally important.

Additionally, such policies and plans will significantly lessen any liability the owners and building managers may attract in the event of a terrorism attack.
